KingShot Minister Scheduler

Instructions: Upload a CSV file with player data to generate minister and advisor schedules. Required columns:

Alliance Player General Speedups General Used For Soldier Training Construction Research TrueGold Pieces Time Slot Start UTC Time Slot End UTC All Times

(Download Blank CSV)
  • Alliance: Player's alliance name (e.g., "MyAlliance")
  • Player: Player's name (e.g., "Player1")
  • General Speedups: Number of general speedups (e.g., 10)
  • General Used For: Categories for general speedups (comma-separated from: Soldier Training, Construction, Research; e.g., "Construction,Research")
  • Soldier Training: Base soldier training speedups (e.g., 5)
  • Construction: Base construction speedups (e.g., 3)
  • Research: Base research speedups (e.g., 2)
  • TrueGold Pieces: Number of TrueGold pieces (e.g., 1)
  • Time Slot Start UTC: Overall start time in HH:MM (e.g., "00:00")
  • Time Slot End UTC: Overall end time in HH:MM (e.g., "23:59")
  • All Times: Available time ranges (comma-separated, supports raw hours like "19-2" for overnight 19:00-23:59 and 00:00-02:00, or full HH:MM like "00:00-12:00,18:00-23:59"; hours are clamped to 0-23)
  • Speedup Allocation: General speedups are distributed based on the 'General Used For' field. Empty field or all three categories (Soldier Training, Construction, Research) → even split among them. Two categories → 60% to the first listed, 40% to the second. One category → all to that category.
  • Player Filtering: Players are included only if they meet the minimum speedups: Construction + Research ≥ minimum for minister roles, or Soldier Training ≥ minimum for advisor roles.
  • Ranking: Players qualifying for minister roles are ranked by the sum of Construction and Research speedups. Players qualifying for advisor roles are ranked by Soldier Training speedups.
  • Scheduling: Players are processed in ranked order. For each player, starting with the first, they are assigned to the earliest available 30-minute slot that fits their time windows if there is one available. (If none are available, they're put on the waiting list.)
